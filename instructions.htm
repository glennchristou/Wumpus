Click on a tab to choose a cave to move to.
If you are near the Wumpus you will smell him!
Click on the Shoot Button to shoot the Wumpus, when he is near.
If you move into a cave with a Wumpus in it, you die!
You only have 3 arrows.
If you are near a pit, you will feel a draft.
If you move into a cave with a pit, you die!
Good luck!
